Clyde's Revolution Documentation

I. Premise/Story

The premise of Clyde's Revolution is that the player must defend his square workforce union from the enemy tribe, Circles of the CEO

II. Gameplay

The gameplay of Clyde's Revolution is similar to a tower defense game where the player can place defensive structures for a cost in gold. Different structures have different functionality such as shooting fast with weak bullets or slowly with string bullets, etc. My thoughts were to give players options as to how they want to approach a battle. As enemy numbers rapidly increase as players progress through levels.

III. Graphical Design

Graphically, I just did not have time to find or create assets for this project, so I reworked my theme into a battle between squares and circles. So, I did my best to utilized shapes and colors to mimick UI and other game details. If I were to have more time, I would try to add custom assets (maybe keep the shape theme and make custom assets)

IV. Processes

For this project, I approached mechanics/ideas one thing at a time. First I incoorperated building selection and a working grid on pixi. I was having issues with getting object on click events working, so I removed the grid and used text labels to spawn in buildings, but the players now have the ability to place buildings anywhere they would like. This was my biggest hurtle to get working, but after that, I would say implementing enemies, bullets, building costs/types, and updaing label were on the easier side of things.
