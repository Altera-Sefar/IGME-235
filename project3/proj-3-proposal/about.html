lV. Story

The story is that the player, the general of a nation, must defend the kingdom's border from enemy nations utilizing different resources at their disposal.

V. Esthetics

The graphics are most likely gonna be pixel art, with sound being 8-bit. I think I will only have sound for placing buildings and hitting enemies (more info in the next section). I think having sound effects for shooting may be too much if the player has a lot of buildings, but testing will determine that.

VI. Mechanics

For my mechanics, I intend for the player to be able to purchase different types of buildings to either defend or produce currency to obtain more buildings/defenses. Powerups or upgrades for buildings would be an interesting idea, but would be a stretch goal. There would also be different kinds of enemies the player could face, which the player must strategize around. There is also the idea of spells the player can cast at the cost of mana (which I am deeming right now as a stretch goal)
