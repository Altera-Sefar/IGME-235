Goal

The goal of my website is to act as a portfolio for recruiters.

For this reason, I wanted to ensure my name and who I am was very visible to the viewer on the top.

Looking back, I should have most likely have put the About Me section above my projects, but it still works

as my intended audience would be reading left to right, and thus reading About me and then my project afterwards.

I also was sure to include links to each of the projects. I put them near the images of gameplay to signify that the

links would bring the viewer to a link where they can play the game after downloading.

Notes

I found this project an interesting experience. The most challenging part for me was making sure my breakpoints

because oh boy mobile screens are a lot bigger than I thought they were. I also did come across issues with images

and responiveness, particularly with sizing and position, but I feel as though I did an okay job. I did have to disable

an image of me with potatoes at lower widths as it negatively affected spacing to the point where there would be empty space

other areas of the page due to the way I sized rows.
